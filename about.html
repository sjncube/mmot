About Us

At Masabatha Mahlo Occupational Therapy, we are dedicated to transforming challenges into triumphs. With years of experience, we offer specialized care in Mental Health, Paediatrics, Physical Rehabilitation, Vocational Rehabilitation, and Medicolegal services to help clients regain independence and achieve their goals.

Masabatha Ncube (B. OCCTHER (UP)): An occupational therapist known for her commitment to excellence, empathy, and holistic care. Masabatha combines expertise and passion to deliver personalized therapy that empowers individuals and families.

Our practice is proudly affiliated with the Health Professions Council of South Africa (HPCSA) and the Occupational Therapy Association of South Africa (OTASA). These affiliations reflect our dedication to upholding the highest professional standards in occupational therapy.
